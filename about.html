About Lari Industries

Lari Industries stands as a beacon of excellence in the safety equipment manufacturing industry. Since our establishment in 2005, we have been dedicated to protecting workers across diverse industries with premium-quality safety products.

For over two decades, Lari Industries has been successfully producing leather safety articles for personnel protection. Our company's unwavering focus on innovation and deep industry experience enables us to offer comprehensive solutions for industrial hand protection. We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of highly competitive and technically advanced products including leather industrial safety gloves, professional shoes and boots, protective aprons, leather jackets for industrial workers, leg guards, and much more.

Our manufacturing philosophy centers on three core pillars: Quality, Innovation, and Safety. Every product that leaves our facility undergoes rigorous testing and quality checks to ensure compliance with national and international safety standards. We understand that when workers trust us with their safety, we carry a responsibility that goes beyond business—we carry the responsibility of protecting lives.
